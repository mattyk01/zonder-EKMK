What's in a name?

Zonder is an intentional misspelling of "sonder," a wonderful and quirky term coined by John Koenig, defined as the "realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own." It serves as a reminder to us to be humble and look carefully, without assumptions.
